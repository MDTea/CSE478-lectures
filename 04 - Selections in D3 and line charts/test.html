About

Dr. Chris Bryan leads the Sonoran Visualization Laboratory (SVL) at Arizona State University. His research focuses on developing novel algorithms, models, techniques, and interfaces to help humans make sense of complex data. Dr. Bryan collaborates with experts across various domains, including medicine, sociology, power systems, and cybersecurity.

Education
